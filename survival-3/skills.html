Information

Abilities are all accessible in the in-game GUI (Right-clicking any Bookshelf Face) providing a user-friendly experience to the "skills" system. These abilities can be obtained through levelling up each individual skill. 
You can also use /skills to access the abilities menu.

To progress your skills and gain knowledge, you can access the advancements menu by pressing ESC → Advancements from which you can view each skill with information on how to level it up. 

Skills and Abilities

Hunter

Adrenaline: Taking damage for most people might
be a problem, but for you its the best
thing to happen! Bonus Damage!

Regeneration: When you get struck, you gain a stack
of regeneration, at the cost of a
MASSIVE hunger debuff.

Nether

Skull Yeet: This skill lets you throw a Wither skull
just like a Wither! This does damage to
the terrain, and entities.

Wither-Resist: When you wear Netherite Armor, you
gain a resistance to wither damage
up to complete negation.

Rift

Access: This allows for you to create an item
called “Reliquary portkey” that allows
you to open ANY container, anywhere.

Blink: Short-ranged, Instantaneous instant
teleportation! Blink Through walls,
or to building tops!

Enderchest: Open an enderchest by holding it in
your hand and clicking! No need to
place this chest down.

Gate: This is your own personal recall object.
Allows you to return to any point after
a short delay.

Resist: Using any Ender ability, or Rift skill
gives you a stack of resistance that
can stack up to 100% resistance.

Unarmed

Glass-Cannon: Just like hunter Adrenaline, this buffs
your damage. based on the lack of
armor that you have on. 0 is max.

Power: This is a flat bonus to Unarmed
damage, and it stacks with all other
damage ramping effects.

Sucker punch: The faster you are moving (sprinting)
the more damage you do in a single
blow.

Seaborne

Oxygen: While underwater you are able to
not consume the air in your lungs
and you can stay underwater longer.

Stealth

Ghost Armor: This passively creates armor that
only lasts for 1-hit then vanishes and
regenerates over time again.

Sight: Gain Night vision when you have
activated Sneak. lasts until you
un-sneak.

Snatch: When you crouch, you suck all nearby
items into your inventory as soon as
you crouch (close-range)

Speed: Gain a speed boost when you sneak.
(Turn off your Screen FOV warp or you
will regret using this)

Ranged

Arrow Recovery: When you land ranged shots, with
this skill you can regenerate the
spent arrows that you shot.

Force: This is a flat damage bonus to all of
your ranged skills, be that a crossbow
or bow, or skill here.

Lunge-Shot: When you shoot an arrow, Reverse
your velocity. IE: 360 no-scope, and Hanzo-jump away.

Piercing: This allows for you to shoot an arrow
that when it hits a Target, it goes right
through and keeps going.

Taming

Damage: This skill increases the raw damage
that all of your tames can do. This only
works on animals that do damage.

Health Boost: This skill increases the raw health of
all of your tames. making them far more
resilient to anything

Health Regen: Tamed animals will have a passive
regeneration buff applied to them
at the moment they become adult.

Pickaxe

Chisel: Instead of mining, you can right click
and get small bits of ore to drop, but
increase the total durability impact.

Veinminer: This allows you to Sneak-mine and mine
all nearby blocks in one go. this does
not bring all blocks to you.

Swords

Machete: This allows for you to chop through
tough foliage, grass, and leaves in
a single swipe!

Agility

Wind up: As you run, you will slowly
but steadily increase in
speed as you sprint away.

Wall-Jump: This skill gives the user the
capability to jump, and latch
onto a wall, and jump again.

Super-jump: This allows the user to crouch
and do a hefty leap into the
air higher than 2+ blocks.

Architect

Foundation: When you crouch, and have no blocks
under you, this creates a magic
platform for you to stand on.

Glass: This is a simple ability that gives the
user’s empty hand the capability to
mine glass with silk touch.

Placement: If you are holding a block type and
sneaky and look at a block, this is a
type of builders wand for you.

Enchanting

Lapis Return: This is a utility for all Enchanters from
Novice to Master this is a critical tool
To give Lapis back when its spent.

XP Return: Every enchantment gives you back
some experience when you craft it
making enchantments cheaper.

Quick Enchant: Enchanted books can now be auto-
bound to items just by clicking the book over the item in your inventory.

Herbalism

Growth-Aura: Passively Grow plants around you,
Spends hunger to do so. but rapidly grows plants.

Hunger-Shield: Spends Hunger to prevent the damage that is done to you. 
Leveling scales hunger consumption.

Replant: You can use a hoe and harvest, then
replant the plants in the area. this is
a useful tool for herbalists.

Crafting

Deconstruction: Using a Smiting Table, ans shears,
you can deconstruct ant item that was
crafted into its base components.

Experience: Gain Experience when crafting, This is
scaled to always be the same amount
per level, but its configurable.

Portable Tables: Receive the ablility to access a crafting table from the palm of your hand.

Discovery

World Armor: As you are walking in the world you
gain a passive armor boost. This
changes based on blocks around.

Experimental Unity: Gain more experience when you get
experience, and each level increases
the amount of xp you gain by a percent.

Experimental Resistance: Consume experience to mitigate the damage you take from all sources

Villager Attraction: Allows for you to get better trades with villagers

Axes

Chop: Axe Chop is a type of lumber axe that
you can use to cut down clusters of
wood.
Ground-Smash: Slam into the ground dealing a small
cluster of damage in an area around you.

Brewing

Lingering: Potions you craft with this skill (scales)
have increased duration, up to 2x at max level.

Super-Heated: This allows for Brewing stands that you
placed to have an increased speed!
Allies also gain this bonus.
