IN-GAME FEATURES

Sewer Side Survival brings many exciting features to Minecraft some of which are exclusive to our server only! Here's some help on how all of those features work!

Money

Money (Balance) can be obtained throughout all areas of the server.

You can find cash from just playing like normal. Jobs, quests, playtime, everything will reward you with money!

Flight

Flight is an essential feature to build large structures or farms.

You can obtain flight using The Exchange or receive daily flight rewards by purchasing a rank. 
You can also send flight to other players using “ /fly give (player) (amount) ” and can check their balance using “ /fly balance (player) ”

Pets

Pets can be obtained randomly from crates and mystery boxes. 

Mystery boxes can be found throughout the world by random chance and will reward a random pet and pet food to level up your pet.

VoteParty (VP)

The VoteParty requires 10 votes to achieve at the start of the month, rewarding everyone online at that moment in time 0.5 WRLD Tokens each! 

It increases by 3 votes every time it's reached and will reset at the end of the month.

Push towards the VoteParty goal by voting using /vote!

Crates

Crates can be earned through hourly playtime rewards along with $WRLD tokens.

Many rewards can be found inside crates such as cosmetics, money, $WRLD & more.

Levels

Levels can be obtained by advancing upon your individual skills 

Every skill milestone you reach, $WRLD tokens will be granted as a reward!

Land Claiming

A land claim is a piece of in-game land which belongs to you. 
You can add people you solely trust to your land claim by using the /trust (name) command. 
You can untrust them by using /untrust (name)

Player Warps

A player warp can be created to allow other players to quickly travel to your location or to set up an area for a specific event / reason. (/pwarp)
Create a warp using the /pwarp set (Warp Name) 

Sponsor slots can be purchased in the player warps menu allowing you to pay a set price to advertise your warp for a specific amount of time.

Bosses

Powerful bosses can be summoned at random throughout The Overworld dimension on the Sewer Side Survival server. 
Bosses can drop a large variety of rewards, each participating member of a boss battle will receive a random reward each.

They have many abilities, so only travel to wherever it was summoned if you're prepared!
